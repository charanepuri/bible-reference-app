History of the Bible in India

52 AD

Thomas the Apostle arrived in India and spread Christianity.

Early Centuries

Scriptures were used in Syriac (Peshitta) in Kerala churches.

1714

First Tamil New Testament printed by Bartholomäus Ziegenbalg.

1800s

William Carey translated Bible into multiple Indian languages.

Modern Era

Bible available in 100+ Indian languages through Bible Society of India.
